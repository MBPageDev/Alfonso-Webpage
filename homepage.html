About Alfonso Brandy
Aged in American oak casks according to the traditional Sherry system of Soleras and Criaderas in Spain, Alfonso is made from selected spirits of good white wines and is healthy and fit for consumption.
Made from careful selection of raw materials obtained from high-strength and medium-strength spirits of good wines.

ALFONSO I
Quality, tradition and nobility are some of the attributes of 'Alfonso', the range of brandies de Jerez (sherry brandies) prepared Bodegas Williams & Humbert, a winery that was founded more than a hundred years ago. The secret of its extraordinary quality lies in the careful selection of raw materials obtained from high-strength and medium-strength spirits of good wines. That is why brandy is considered the most noble of spirits.
